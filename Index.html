About BeeRad LLC

BeeRad LLC is your trusted partner for professional mobile equipment repair and diagnostics services in Lubbock, Texas. With years of experience, we provide fast, reliable, and expert service for all your heavy equipment needs.

How BeeRad Became BeeRad

BeeRad was built on family, hard work, and a vision for the future. The name 'BeeRad' is a combination inspired by our children's names—a daily reminder of why we push ourselves. After years of hands-on experience in the heavy equipment industry, we took the leap to build something of our own.

Our goal is to create a lasting legacy for our family and to show our children that dreams can come true through dedication, integrity, and perseverance. Every repair we make and every customer we serve reflects the values that BeeRad was founded on: honest work, dependable service, and a commitment to doing the job right.
